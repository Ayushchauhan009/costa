About Us

Costa Cruises, is a renowned Italian cruising company with over 75 years of experience. Coming for the first time to India, Costa offers unparalleled Italian hospitality, delightful cuisine, exquisite shopping, and a vast selection of famous Italian brands. With 19,000 passionate associates worldwide, Costa Cruises creates unforgettable holiday experiences through 137 itineraries, 261 destinations, and 60 embarkation ports across Europe, Middle East, South America, Asia, and now Incredible India..
